  • How do I gain access to the benefits of the Razer Education Purchase Program?

    You must be a current student, employed teacher, or employed staff of an eligible university, college, technical school, or high school in the participating countries. You are required to register an account with us using a valid education email address.

  • Do I need to use a coupon code at checkout?

    No. Our program’s special pricing will already be applied to applicable products after you have signed in or registered with your education email address.

  • Does the special pricing apply to all Razer products?

    No, it only applies to selected products and is only applicable for purchases made on the razer.com page of your specified country. Please read our terms and conditions below for more information.

  • Can I redeem the student discount at physical RazerStores?

    Yes, you can enjoy our education benefits at physical RazerStores. Simply present your education email address or verification card to our store staff.

  • Can I use promo codes on top of the special pricing?

    No – Promo codes cannot be stacked on top of other promotions.

  • I’m unsure if my educational institution is eligible for the discount. What should I do?

    You can submit a request to us via email to [email protected]. However, requests are subject to approval at the discretion of Razer.

  • How many units of the same item can I purchase with special pricing?

    You may purchase up to 2 units of the same item within one calendar month.

  • I would like to purchase products in bulk. How do I express my interest?

    For corporate bulk purchase, please send us your request via email to [email protected].

  • Can I customize Razer products?

    Please send us an idea of your design and the product of interest via email to [email protected].
